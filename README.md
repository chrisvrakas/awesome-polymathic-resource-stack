* **Privacy & Security** (OpSec, encryption, anonymity, Bitcoin)

* **Philosophy & Psychology** (how to think, not just what to think)

* **Culture & History** (true crime, biographies, underground narratives)

This list is my ever-evolving cognitive fingerprint‚Äîeach item reflects personal experience, tested workflows, and hard-won knowledge. And I'm happy to share it with you.
